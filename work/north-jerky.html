North Jerky

— local, plant-based jerky that packs as much flavour and protein as meat does. Ready to power your next northern adventure.

Year

2018

Scope

  • Branding
  • Packaging
  • Copywriting
  • Website

Solution

The duo of brothers behind North Jerky had a specific instruction: they don't want to be a 'vegan' brand. Their plant-based nature is an added bonus. The real pro is in their flavour and protein power. North Jerky is meant to fuel adventure, play and sustainable exploration. With that in mind, we set out to create a brand inspired by some of the ultimate explorers: scouts. Using elements from highway signs and scout badges, we created a brand chockful of illustrations that sits just as well at a MEC store as it would at Whole Foods, and gives the North Jerky team plenty of merchandising opportunities.
