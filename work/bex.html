BEX Interiors

brings together contemporary design and materials with mid-century inspiration to create some of the finest kitchen cabinetry in Ottawa.

Year

2017

Scope

  • Branding
  • Stationery
  • Website

Solution

The BEX brand needed to speak to the liveliness of their team and versatility of their design. It also needed to be simple to manage and apply. Using a simple quadrant representing an open cabinet door, we created a logo and a playful and variable pattern that is as modular as the kitchens they create. The understated retro palette and choice of Clarendon as a headline font bring in that touch of mid-century influence, all in a simple, gridded application that keeps the brand modern and elegant.
