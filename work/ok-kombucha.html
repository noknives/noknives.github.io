OK Kombucha

is where flavour innovation meets health-conscious beverage; and they needed a brand that matched the playfulness of their choices.

Year

2018

Scope

  • Branding
  • Packaging
  • Collateral

Solution

Led by an adventurous duo out of Toronto, OK! Kombucha set their sights on not just being a health beverage, but a fun, non-alcoholic soda alternative that's also good for your insides. At Salt, we drew inspiration from the inventive flavour combinations and the natural fizziness of the beverage to create a playful, swooping brand that stands out at coffee shops, grocery stores or lounges — day or night.
